About Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Who We Are

Hope For Kids Ministries is a dedicated organization committed to transforming the lives of orphaned and vulnerable children in Mityana, Uganda. We provide shelter, food, education, healthcare, and emotional support to give every child a chance at a brighter future.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive care and support to orphaned and vulnerable children, empowering them with education, healthcare, and love to become productive members of society.

Our Vision

A community where every child has access to quality education, healthcare, and a safe environment to grow and thrive.

Our Values

  • Compassion for every child
  • Integrity and transparency in all we do
  • Accountability to donors and communities
  • Dignity, safety, and respect for all

Our Impact

Real change in the lives of children

Education Access

Over 120 children enrolled in school with fees, uniforms, and supplies fully covered each year.

Safe Shelter

Daily meals, clean bedding, and secure housing for children who would otherwise be on the streets.

Healthcare

Regular medical checkups, immunizations, and emergency treatment for every child in our care.

Community Outreach

Food, clothing, and counseling support extended to vulnerable families across Mityana District.

Our Programs

Comprehensive Care for Children

Safe Housing

We provide safe, clean, and comfortable homes where children can sleep peacefully and feel secure.

Nutritious Meals

Three balanced meals daily to ensure proper nutrition and healthy development.

Education

Access to quality education with school fees, uniforms, and learning materials provided.

Healthcare

Medical checkups, immunizations, and treatment for illnesses are available to all children.

Counseling & Support

Emotional and psychological support to help children heal from trauma and build resilience.

Skills Training

Vocational training and life skills programs to prepare children for independent futures.
