Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Hope For Kids Ministries ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you visit our website, donate, volunteer, or contact us.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact information: Name, email, phone number when you fill out forms
  • Donation information: Amount and payment reference details you provide
  • Communications: Messages you send us via email, WhatsApp, or contact forms
  • Technical data: Basic browser and device information through essential cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process donations and send receipts or confirmations
  • Respond to inquiries about volunteering, sponsorship, or partnerships
  • Send newsletters and updates (only if you subscribe)
  • Improve our website and communications
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Children's Privacy

We take child protection seriously. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website. Photos and stories of children in our care are shared only with appropriate consent and safeguarding measures in place.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with trusted service providers (such as payment processors) who help us operate, or when required by law.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at hopeforkidsministries@gmail.com.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to remember your cookie consent preference. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
